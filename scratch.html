Spanish & Cultural Exchange

Learning Spanish changed how I see the world. After two years living and serving across Spanish-speaking communities, I came to love not just the language but the cultura behind it — the food, the music, and the people. This page is a small celebration of that.

Where Spanish Is Spoken

Spanish is an official language in 20+ countries. A few I find fascinating:

  1. Spain — where the language began
    • Flamenco music and dance
    • La Tomatina festival
  2. Mexico — the most Spanish speakers of any country
    • Día de los Muertos
    • Incredible regional cuisine
  3. Argentina
    • Tango
    • Distinctive "vos" grammar

Why It Matters to Me

Being bilingual isn't just a skill on a résumé — it rewires how you think and connects you to hundreds of millions of people. A few things I gained:

  • Confidence leading and mentoring people from many backgrounds
  • Patience and empathy from living inside a different culture
  • A lifelong love of learning — ¡nunca pares!
